      Idly is a traditional South Indian dish made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. It is a good source of carbohydrates and protein, while being low in fat, making it a nutritious and easily digestible breakfast option.

    • Tomato Kuruma

      • Tomato Kuruma is a flavorful South Indian curry made with tomatoes, coconut, and spices. This vegan dish offers a rich blend of tangy tomatoes and aromatic spices, providing a tasty and nutritious accompaniment to rice or Indian breads.

    • Wheat Rava Uppuma

      • Wheat Rava Uppuma is a popular South Indian dish made from wheat semolina (rava). It's a nutritious and savory dish cooked with roasted wheat rava, vegetables, and spices, offering a wholesome breakfast or snack option.
